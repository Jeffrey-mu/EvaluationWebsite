Introduction

Evaluation Station is committed to providing the real product experience to our customers and the public. Please contact rose.kaye@ssnssn.com to get more support from us.


Online Accessibility

We thrives to bring users with accessible digital experience. If a disabled user has accessibility issues with our website, product or services, don’t hesitate to send us email at rose.kaye@ssnssn.com. In that email, please notify us the accessibility difficulty, the website address of the product or service.


Third-Party Websites

The products and services may contain third-party website links. We shall not ensure you the accessibility of the third-party websites, and shall not remediate the accessibility issue of those third-party websites.


Reasonable Accommodations

Individuals should send an email to rose.kaye@ssnssn.com if they need a reasonable accommodation to access Evaluation Station’s service and information. Those requesters should leave the contact information like email address or telephone number that can be reached.We may provide reasonable accommodation in case of the situation.


Feedback


We wish all the products and services are accessible to all our customers. If you still seek for accessibility support, please contact rose.kaye@ssnssn.com.
